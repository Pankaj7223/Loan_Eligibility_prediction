Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our loan eligibility prediction system.

How accurate is the loan prediction?
Our model is trained on a vast dataset of historical loan applications and achieves an accuracy rate of approximately 98%. However, this is a preliminary assessment and final approval depends on the bank's verification process.
Does checking my eligibility affect my credit score?
No. Our tool performs a "soft inquiry" based on the data you provide. It does not communicate with credit bureaus, so your credit score remains completely unaffected.
Is my personal data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to process your data. We do not store your personal financial details after the session ends, and we never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent.
What documents do I need for the actual loan?
While requirements vary by lender, you typically need proof of identity, proof of address, income statements (salary slips or tax returns), and bank statements for the last 3-6 months.
Why was my loan prediction "Not Eligible"?
Common reasons include a low credit history, insufficient income relative to the loan amount, or lack of collateral. Our system considers multiple factors. We recommend improving your credit score or applying for a lower amount.
